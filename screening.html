Freshers Screening Guide

Welcome to the University of Uyo Data Science Freshers Guide. This page explains everything you need to complete before your departmental screening.

Read Guide

Step 1 - Pay Your Acceptance Fee

After accepting your admission on JAMB CAPS, the first payment is your acceptance fee. Visit the UNIUYO student portal, generate your payment invoice (Remita), pay through any approved bank or online channel, then print your payment receipt.

Keep multiple photocopies of the receipt because you may need them during clearance and registration.

Step 2 - Complete Online Registration

Log into the university portal with your admission details and complete every required section carefully.

  • Upload your passport photograph.
  • Fill in your personal information.
  • Verify your O'Level results.
  • Print all generated forms.

Step 3 - Documents Required for Screening

Prepare both the originals and several photocopies of the following:

  • JAMB Admission Letter
  • JAMB Result Slip
  • Acceptance Fee Receipt
  • School Fees Receipt (if already paid)
  • Birth Certificate or Age Declaration
  • Certificate of Local Government of Origin
  • O'Level Result (WAEC, NECO or NABTEB)
  • Passport Photographs
  • Online Registration Forms
  • Student Bio-data Form
  • NIN Slip (if requested)

Step 4 - Departmental Screening

On the screening day, arrive early with all your documents neatly arranged inside a file.

Your documents will be checked and verified before your clearance is approved.

Who Conducts the Screening?

Screening is usually coordinated by officials from the Faculty of Computing together with the Department of Data Science and designated administrative staff.

Always follow the latest instructions issued by your department because the assigned officers may change each academic session.

Where is the Screening Held?

The venue is announced by the department or faculty before the screening exercise. It is commonly held within the Faculty of Computing or another designated university venue.

Always confirm the official venue from the departmental notice board, faculty office, or the university portal before your screening date.

Helpful Tips

  • Make at least five photocopies of every important document.
  • Keep all originals safe inside a transparent file.
  • Arrive at least one hour before the scheduled time.
  • Dress decently.
  • Do not laminate important documents.
  • Bring a pen and a small notebook.

Final Advice

Congratulations on your admission into the University of Uyo. Complete every payment and registration on time, keep your documents organized, and always rely on official university announcements for any updates.
