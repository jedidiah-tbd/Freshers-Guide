How to Pay Your School Fees

Follow this complete guide to pay your UNIUYO school fees correctly and avoid common mistakes.

Start Reading

Before You Begin

Before paying your school fees, ensure that:

  • Your admission has been accepted on JAMB CAPS.
  • You have paid your acceptance fee.
  • Your student portal account has been activated.
  • You have a working email address and phone number.
  • You have an ATM card or access to a bank for payment.

Step 1 - Log Into the Student Portal

Visit the official UNIUYO student portal and log in using your registration number and password.

Confirm that all your personal details are correct before proceeding.

Step 2 - Generate Your School Fees Invoice

Navigate to the School Fees Payment section and generate your Remita Retrieval Reference (RRR) or payment invoice.

Double-check your name, department, level and session before making payment.

Step 3 - Make Your Payment

You can pay:

  • Online using your ATM card.
  • Through an approved commercial bank.
  • Using any approved electronic payment platform listed on the student portal.

Wait until the transaction is completed successfully before closing your browser.

Step 4 - Print Your Receipt

After successful payment, return to the student portal and print your official school fees receipt.

Print at least one copy because you will need them during submission of your four-files, and some other departmental registration.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Do not pay using another student's invoice.
  • Always verify your name before payment.
  • Never pay into a personal bank account.
  • Do not leave the payment page before confirmation.
  • Always keep your payment receipt.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if my payment does not reflect?

Wait for some time and refresh your portal. If the payment still does not appear, contact the ICT Support Unit or the Bursary with your payment receipt.

Can I pay in installments?

Follow the current university payment policy for your academic session. Check the student portal or official announcements for any updates.

Should I keep my receipt?

Yes. Keep both digital and printed copies of every receipt throughout your stay in the university.

Helpful Tips

  • Use a stable internet connection.
  • Screenshot every successful payment.
  • Save your Remita receipt.
  • Print at least one copy of every payment receipt.
  • Store digital copies in Google Drive or your email.
