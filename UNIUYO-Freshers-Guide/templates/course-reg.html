First Semester Course Registration

This guide explains how Data Science freshers can register their first-semester courses and avoid common registration mistakes.

Read Guide

What is Course Registration?

Course registration is the process of selecting the courses you will study during the semester through the University of Uyo student portal. Every student must complete this process before the registration deadline.

Failure to register your courses may prevent you from writing examinations, so it is important to complete your registration on time.

Before You Register

Make sure you have completed the following:

  • Accepted your admission.
  • Paid your acceptance fee.
  • Paid your school fees.
  • Completed your departmental screening.
  • Activated your student portal account.

How to Register Your Courses

  1. Log in to the UNIUYO Student Portal.
  2. Navigate to the Course Registration section.
  3. Select the appropriate academic session and semester.
  4. Tick all approved first-semester courses.
  5. Review your selected courses carefully.
  6. Submit your registration.
  7. Print at least three copies of your Course Registration Form.

After Registration

  • Print a copy of your Course Registration Form.
  • Submit the cop for signing by your department.
  • make photocopies of the signed copy and keep safe.
  • Save a PDF copy on your phone or computer.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Do not skip any compulsory course.
  • Always confirm the correct academic session.
  • Do not wait until the registration deadline.
  • Check every course code before submitting.
  • Print your registration slip immediately after submission.
