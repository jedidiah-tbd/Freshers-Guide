Find important locations around the University of Uyo Main Campus. Click any image to view it in full size.

Faculty of Computing

The Faculty of Computing is the most important building to the data science student, because most of the academic processing are made here.

Location

the faculty of computing can be easily located at the computing bus stop, at university of uyo main campus. the image here shows what the building looks like from the front view.

University Library

The library provides thousands of textbooks, journals and research materials for students. It is one of the best places for reading and preparing for examinations.

Location

Located close to the faculty of biological sciences. Carry your library card whenever you visit.

ICT Centre

The ICT Centre is used for CBT examinations.

Location

A short distance from the Faculty of Computing, right after ict2 building, agric bus stop. Always arrive early during examination periods.

Senate Building

The Senate Building houses several administrative offices where important university decisions and documentation are handled.

Location

Situated in the administrative section of the campus, at the Y-junction after entering the UNIUYO main campus.
